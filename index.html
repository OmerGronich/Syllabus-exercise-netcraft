Full Stack Development Course | Syllabus

This is a documentation of the syllabus for the Full Stack Development course led by Rotem Meiri and Tidhar Zeifer as part of the very first assignment.

The Netcraft Full Stack Development course is a professional training course in "Web Development" - the most in-demand field of modern development. The course graduates find jobs in leading international companies such as:  Wix, Microsoft, Hp, Intel and more... At the end of this course you will know how to build rich and efficient Web interfaces, apps and fully responsive websites that support HTML 5, working with servers and databases in JavaScript. This course fully prepares you for potential job interviews.

About Netcraft

Founded in the early 2000s, Netcraft offers design and development services. The company has rich experience working with web entrepreneurs and start-up companies. The company's main customers include: Amdocs, Nice, PayPal, Intel, Dell, Check Point, eBay, Google, Teva, Partner, Bank Hapoalim, Netafim, Direct Insurance, The Land Group, Menorah Insurers and more.

About Netcraft Academy

Netcraft Academy,  the School of User Experience was founded out of our passion and desire to share the knowledge and experience we accumulate in the field of user experience on a daily basis. The school offers a variety of courses in which you can experiment and learn from the leading professionals in the field on correct working methodologies, client stories from the Israeli and global market and actual experience in the leading tools used to characterize and design interfaces in the user experience world.
