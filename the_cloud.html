The Cloud

Let's say we finished our software project: We developed a spectacular interface and built all the server-side logic using all the modern tools, but how do you allow other people to use the software / app? To that end, we will understand what the "cloud" means and how it helps us to make our product public. We'll bring real-world examples by big companies like Netflix, Twitter and find solutions to common problems. As part of the chapter, a free Amazon cloud account was opened and used for hosting and application service built during the course.
